1. Create two topics:

 ./kafka-topics --create --bootstrap-server localhost:9092 --topic LeftStreamTopic --partitions 10
 
 ./kafka-topics --create --bootstrap-server localhost:9092 --topic RightStreamTopic --partitions 10

 ./kafka-topics --create --bootstrap-server localhost:9092 --topic JoinedTopic --partitions 10


2. Run examples.streams.JoinStringsExample
  
  The application must be running
  
  
3. Run JoinedStringExampleDriver. This will generate values into LeftStreamTopic and RightStreamTopic


4. Check the topic JoinedTopic
